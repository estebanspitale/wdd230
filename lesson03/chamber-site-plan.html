The Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Villa Carlos Paz Chamber of Commerce and Tourism

"I selected this name because of the name of the city and because of the objective of the chamber of commerce and tourism to promote and develop these activities in the city."

Site Purpose

The main purposes of our website are:

1. In the commercial area, publicize the businesses that are within the city by providing them with advertising and public knowledge spaces.

2. In the tourism area, it will provide information about the tourist attractions that the city has with their respective information, including accommodation and gastronomy.

Scenarios

Some possible scenarios that will be addressed for the hearing:

  • "What events will the chamber hold in the low season that promote commerce?"
  • "Where can I find information about the location and contact of the Chamber's Board of Directors?"
  • "What are the measures that have been carried out to promote trade and tourism?"

Color Schema

I will use the following colors:

  • Primary Color: #1359a4 - (color used for titles, important boxes)
  • Secondary Color: #c2eeff - (color used for some backgrounds)

Typography

I will use the following fonts:

  • Verdana - Headings
  • sans-serif - Body text

(These are some of the fonts and typography that I will start with. It is subject to future changes.)

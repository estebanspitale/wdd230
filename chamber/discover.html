Demographic statistics

The 2022 National Census of Population, Households and Housing gave Villa Carlos Paz the figure of 96,000 inhabitants

Information about Villa Carlos Paz

  • DATE OF FOUNDATION: July 16, 1913.
  • DATE THAT WAS DECLARED A CITY: July 16, 1964.
  • DATE OF REAFFIRMATION AS A TOURIST CITY: July 16, 1987.
  • PATRON'S FEAST: July 16: Feast of Our Lady of Carmen - Vice-Patron San Roque
  • HEIGHT ABOVE SEA LEVEL: 650 meters.
  • TYPICAL FLOWER: Plumerillo or Goatbeard
